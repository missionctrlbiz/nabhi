Got Questions? We've Got Answers

What is NABHI?

NABHI stands for Nurses Across the Borders Humanitarian Initiative, an NGO committed to providing humanitarian aid and improving global healthcare systems. Established in 1999, NABHI focuses on addressing healthcare disparities and delivering essential services to underserved communities, particularly in regions affected by crises and disasters.

How can I get involved with NABHI?

You can get involved with NABHI in several impactful ways:

  • Volunteer: Contribute your time and skills by participating in our healthcare programs, training workshops, and community outreach initiatives.
  • Donate: Support our mission financially to help fund our various programs and initiatives. 
  • Partner: We welcome partnerships with other NGOs, healthcare providers, and corporate entities.
Where does NABHI operate?

NABHI operates internationally, focusing on areas that suffer from inadequate healthcare facilities, crises, and disasters. Our operations span multiple countries, targeting regions with the greatest need for humanitarian aid and healthcare interventions. 

 

How does NABHI address climate change and its impact on health?

NABHI addresses climate change through advocacy, education, and action. As the UNFCCC focal point NGO in Nigeria, we lead campaigns to raise awareness about the health impacts of climate change and promote sustainable environmental practices.

How can organizations partner with NABHI?

Organizations can partner with NABHI in various ways to support our mission and enhance our programs. Partnerships can include financial support, collaborative projects, resource sharing, and joint advocacy efforts. We welcome partnerships with other NGOs, healthcare providers, corporations, and governmental bodies.
